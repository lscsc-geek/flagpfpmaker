Overview

Welcome to Flag PFP Maker ("we," "our," or "this website"). We value your privacy highly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information you provide when using our website and services.

By using our website, you accept and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We do not store your personal information. Flag PFP Maker is a purely frontend tool, and all processing happens locally in your browser.

When you use our website, the following information may be processed (but is not stored on our servers):

  • Images you upload to create profile pictures
  • Flag selections and custom settings you choose

We do not send your uploaded images to our servers; all image processing happens locally in your browser.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies to collect user information.

Use of Information

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, we do not use your information for any purposes such as marketing, analytics, or sharing with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We recommend that you review the privacy policy of any third-party website you visit.

Data Security

All your data processing happens locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. This means the security of your uploaded images and created profile pictures is entirely under your control.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any Privacy Policy changes on this page and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
