Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Flag PFP Maker

What is Flag PFP Maker?

+

Flag PFP Maker is a free tool for creating custom profile pictures with flag overlays. It's perfect for showing support or expressing identity.

How do I upload my own image?

+

Click the "Choose New Image" button and select your image file, or simply drag and drop your image onto the upload area.

What's the difference between Ring and Background mode?

+

Ring mode places the flag in a ring around your image, while Background mode places the flag behind your image.

Can I use my own custom flag?

+

Yes! Click the "Custom Image" button to upload your own flag image with Flag PFP Maker.

Is Flag PFP Maker free to use?

+

Yes, Flag PFP Maker is completely free to use without any restrictions.
